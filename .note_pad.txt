@=========================================================================@>Motions<@=========================================================================@
Mais Alguns Motions
Ctrl + u: Scroll up (half a page).
Ctrl + d: Scroll down (half a page).
Ctrl + b: Scroll up (full page).
Ctrl + f: Scroll down (full page).
m{letter}: Set a mark at the current cursor position.
'{letter}``: Jump to the line of the specified mark.
`{letter}: Jump to the exact position of the specified mark.
%: Move to the matching parenthesis/brace.
